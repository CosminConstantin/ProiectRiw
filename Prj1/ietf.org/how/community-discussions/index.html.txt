Community discussions often take place outside the IETF Working Group processes. They can take several forms, including email lists, Birds of a Feather sessions at IETF meetings, and focused workshops. Workshops Workshops are often more structured discussions about a topic of interest to the IETF community Non-Working group email lists These email lists are used to discuss particular topics of interest to the IETF community. Birds of a Feather This page provides information about Birds of a Feather sessions (BoFs)--initial, informal, in-person discussions about a particular the topic of interest to the IETF community--and related policies and procedures.
