Meeting Discussion Lists Each IETF meeting has an attendee discussion list where attendees can connect with each other regarding issues related to the meeting. Attendees are given the opportunity to subscribe to this mailing list when they register for the meeting. To subscribe to the current meeting discussion list or view the list archives, please visit the following links: 101attendees Subscription 101attendees email archive IETF Social Email Lists IETF Runners Mailing List Discussion list for finding good routes around IETF meeting venues for running, and for sharing running-related experiences with IETF participants. To subscribe to the IETF runners mailing list or view the list archives, please visit the following links: ietf-runners (Subscription) ietf-runners email archive IETF Travel Companions A mailing list has been set up for companions of IETF attendees. This mailing list can be used to get in contact with other companions traveling to an IETF meeting in order to organize day trips, lunch, dinner or simply just get together while at an IETF meeting.
