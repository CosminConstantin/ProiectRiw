Interim Meetings The work of the IETF is largely conducted over mailing lists; however, some working groups also occasionally hold "interim" meetings, either face-to-face or virtually via conference calls. Past Meetings IETF meetings are held three times a year. The first IETF meeting was held in San Diego in January, 1986.
