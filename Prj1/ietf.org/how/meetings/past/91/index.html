IETF 91 Honolulu

9 Nov 2014 - 14 Nov 2014

IETF 91 took place in Honolulu, Hawaii from November 9-14, 2014. This page is provided for informational purposes only; links may be outdated or broken.

    IETF Meetings start Monday morning and run through Friday afternoon (13:30), with late scheduling changes. Newcomers' training and technical tutorials take place the previous Sunday afternoon. Participants should plan their travel accordingly. IETF 91 Took place from 9 to 11 November 2014 in Honolulu. For more information, see the welcome and summary posts on the IETF Blog.
