IETF 100 Social Event

14 Nov 2017

The IETF 100 social event is hosted by Cisco and will be held at the S.E.A. Aquarium in Singapore on November 14, 2017.

    At the IETF 100 social event you will explore the marine realm of S.E.A. Aquarium, home to more than 100,000 marine animals of over 800 species, across into 49 different habitats, each one as fascinating as the next. Transportation by bus will be provided. Approximate travel time to the S.E.A. aquarium is 30 minutes.

    Date: Tuesday, 14 November, 2017
    Time: 19:00 to 22:00

    A map of the Aquarium may be found HERE.

    Cost: $35 per ticket, limit to two per attendee. Children require a ticket.

    Availability: 600

    Food and beverage: Enjoy an international buffet dinner, beer, wine and non-alcoholic beverages. To view the menu for the social event, click HERE.

    To purchase a ticket to the IETF 100 social event CLICK HERE, or purchase on-site at the IETF registration desk, subject to availability.

    Note: You must first register for IETF 100 to purchase a social ticket. 

    The IETF 100 Social Event will take place at the exciting S.E.A. Aquarium!

    Venue
    S.E.A. Aquarium
    Resorts World Sentosa
    8 Sentosa Gateway, Sentosa Island
