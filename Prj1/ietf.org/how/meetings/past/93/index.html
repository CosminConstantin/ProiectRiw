IETF 93 Prague

19 Jul 2015 - 24 Jul 2015

IETF 93 took place in Prague, Czech Republic, from July 19-24, 2015. This page is provided for informational purposes only; links may be outdated or broken.

    IETF Meetings start Monday morning and run through Friday afternoon (13:30), with late scheduling changes. Newcomers' training and technical tutorials take place the previous Sunday afternoon. Participants should plan their travel accordingly. IETF 93 took place in Prague, Czech Republic, from July 19-24, 2015. For more information, see the welcome and summary posts on the IETF Blog.
