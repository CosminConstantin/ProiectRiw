IETF 94 Yokohama

1 Nov 2015 - 6 Nov 2015

IETF 94 took place in Yokohama, Japan, from November 1-6, 2015. This page is provided for informational purposes only; links may be outdated or broken.

    IETF Meetings start Monday morning and run through Friday afternoon (13:30), with late scheduling changes. Newcomers' training and technical tutorials take place the previous Sunday afternoon. Participants should plan their travel accordingly. IETF 94 took place in Yokohama, Japan, from November 1-6, 2015. For more information, see the summary post on the IETF Blog.
