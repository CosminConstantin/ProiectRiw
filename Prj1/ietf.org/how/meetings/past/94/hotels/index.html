    Cancelation: Individuals can cancel a reservation without penalty until 3 days prior to check-in/arrival. In case of cancelation less than 3 days prior to check-in/arrival or non-arrival or no-show the hotel may charge the individual one night's stay as a cancelation fee.

    Guest Substitution: Guests may substitute names for reserved rooms without penalty.

    Overflow Hotels

    NOTE: Reservations for all overflow hotels are being managed through the Japanese Travel Bureau (JTB) Corporate Sales, Inc.

    Reservations link for all overflow hotels: https://amarys-jtb.jp/ietf94/

    Deposit: After you complete your registration, please click the "Go to Payment Page" button. Payments by credit card will be accepted. The due date for the deposit payment is 23 October, 2015. Please note that payments will not be accepted after this date. In order to confirm your hotel reservation a deposit of JPY 5,000 is required. The deposit will be deducted from the total bill upon check-out from the hotel. Receipts will be provided by the hotel upon check-out for the full amount (including the deposit).

    Reservations cut-off date for ALL Overflow Hotels: October 23 2015 at 12:00 Japan Standard Time (GMT +9)

    Cancelation for ALL Overflow Hotels: No charge to the individual will be made if cancelations are requested 10 or more days prior to the check-in date. After this date, the following schedule for cancelations will be followed.

    2 - 9 Days prior to check-in10% of total room rate1 Day prior to check-in20% of total room rateOn the day of check-in80% of total room rateNo-show/non-arrival100% of total room rate
