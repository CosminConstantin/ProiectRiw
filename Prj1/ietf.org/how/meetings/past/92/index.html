IETF 92 Dallas

22 Mar 2015 - 27 Mar 2015

IETF 92 took place in Dallas, Texas from March 22-27 2015. This page is provided for informational purposes only. links may be outdated or broken.

    IETF Meetings start Monday morning and run through Friday afternoon (13:30), with late scheduling changes. Newcomers' training and technical tutorials take place the previous Sunday afternoon. Participants should plan their travel accordingly. IETF 92 took place in Dallas, Texas from March 22-27 2015. For more information, see the welcome and summary posts on the IETF Blog.
