Held in conjunction with IETF meetings, Bits-n-Bites events take place in social settings designed to encourage in-depth discussions. With complimentary hor d’oeuvres, wine and craft beers, this dynamic two-hour event is eagerly anticipated by the entire IETF community. Companies and organizations often work together in teams to demonstrate interoperability and the practical implementations of IETF standards. Participant Benefits: IETF Bits-n-Bites provides the opportunity to showcase products and solutions to technology leaders from around the world in the context of a week-long meeting focused on technical development and evolution. Participants at previous IETF Bits-n- Bites held in Asia, Europe, and the North America have showcased technologies related to the Internet of Things (IoT), security and privacy, Real-time Web communications, and software defined networking (SDN). As a participant in an IETF Bits-n-Bites, your company will receive: A 6-foot draped expo table, configured with electrical power and hardwired into the IETF’s high bandwidth Internet Company or organization logo on the IETF website Two slides of a rotating slide display in the room for the duration of the event Company or organization logo on signage throughout the meeting space Acknowledgement at the IETF meeting plenary and in the proceedings of the meeting Learn More For more information about how to participate in an upcoming Bits-n-Bites event, please contact us at sponsorship@ietf.org.
