IETF 101 London

17 Mar 2018 - 23 Mar 2018

IETF 101 starts Saturday 17 March and runs through Friday afternoon, 23 March.

    The IETF Hackathon and IETF Codesprint take place on the weekend. Newcomers' training and technical tutorials take place on Sunday afternoon. Participants should plan their travel accordingly. The schedule may be updated very close to the start to the meeting.
