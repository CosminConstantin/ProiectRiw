In this section Hide in this section Running code IETF Hackathons Code Sprint CodeStand Implementation reports Key Info The IETF Hackathons aim to: Advance the pace and relevance of IETF standards activities by bringing the speed and collaborative spirit of open source development into the IETF Bring developers and young people into IETF and get them exposed to and interested in IETF Past IETF Hackathons have covered a range of topics including; DNS, HTTP 2.0, NETVC, OpenDaylight, ONOS, VPP/FD.io, RiOT, SFC, TLS 1.3, WebRTC, YANG/NETCONF/RESTCONF. New technologies are always encouraged. The next IETF Hackathon will be held in London, March 17-18, 2018.
